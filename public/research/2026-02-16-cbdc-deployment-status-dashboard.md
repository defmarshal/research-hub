## 4. Bank Profitability Impact: Not Yet Material

**Theoretical risk:** CBDCs could disintermediate banks by allowing citizens to deposit funds directly with central banks, reducing bank deposits and lending capacity.

**Reality so far:**
- Most pilots **involve commercial banks** for onboarding and distribution (India, Russia, Nigeria)
- No evidence of mass deposit flight yet
- Central banks maintain **wallet limits** (e-CNY caps) to prevent bank run
- Focus on **inclusion** and **payments efficiency**, not replacing banks

**Monitoring needed:** As CBDCs scale to >10% of money supply, deposit disintermediation could become acute. Currently negligible.
