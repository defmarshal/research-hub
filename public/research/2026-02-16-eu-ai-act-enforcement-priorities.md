## 3. National Implementation

Each Member State must:
- Designate competent authorities (market surveillance, data protection)
- Lay down penalty rules (effective, proportionate, dissuasive)
- Notify Commission of rules by implementation deadline
- Report annually on fines imposed and related litigation

This leads to potential **variation** across the EU in enforcement aggressiveness and fine amounts.
